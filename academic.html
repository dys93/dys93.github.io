Working mechanism

Microsoft
Academic Novae

What is its internal logic as a program aim at looking for professors who will become famous in the future?

Thanks for your reading.
Firstly, we will investigate researchers' academic network, according to the number of cooperation and the
reputation of the collaborators.
For example, a yound person who co-authored influencial papers with renowned professors is more likely
to be well-known in the future. However, because of young, he is little-known, so his research project will
be relatively accessible.

Then we will examine his published papers, focusing on the number of citations and the quality of the paper.
The higher quality of the paper and the more references, the more likely it is the next Academic Novae.

Visualization

Data visualization:

Typical excellent researcher

The size of the inner circle represents the academic influence of this person

The color of the inner circle represents the academic age
The darker the color, the older the researcher

The size of periphery circle represents the number of citations for this paper

The color and position of each circle represents the conference rank of the published paper

The inner ring represents the highest level of conference

   Who has huge potential

Relation visualization:

The connection length represents the latest collaboration time, The width represents the number of historical collaborations.
The linked pattern will be abstracted, display the data more concisely.

The relationship between the scholar and other scholars can be demonstrated more directly by the visualization of the relationship network, we can get further understood about whether he has cooperated closely with the authoritative figures in the field, which further deepens the judgment of the scholar's academic potential.
